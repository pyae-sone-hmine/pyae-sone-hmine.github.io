About Me

I am a student at MIT studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Finance. I am passionate about building intelligent systems that bridge the gap between AI and the physical world. My current work focuses on reinforcement learning and dexterous manipulation.

Apart from school and research, I work at Magna International, a leading mobility company, where I support the R&D division in advanced robotics and work directly with executives to spearhead innovative technology and AI initiatives. Outside of labs and work meetings, you’ll probably find me painting, traveling, exploring nature, or deep in a good book.
